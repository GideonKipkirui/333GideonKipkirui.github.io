HANNIBAL - THE BATTLE OF CANNAE

After defeating the Romas at the Battle of Lake Trasimene, Hannibal now moved his forces down south. There was panic in Rome and Quintus Fabius Maximus was appointed dictator to deal with Hannibal. Fabius , however , refused to meet hannibal in the open field and this strategy became highly unpopular in Rome. Romans was use to attacking, not hidding. The Romans was forced to go on the offensive after Hannibal positioned his position between Rome and it's vital grain supply. This setup the epic Battle of cannae. Here Hannibal defeated the Romans inflicting the worst single day defeat in Rome's history.

INDEXES

Index is a Latin word that means “he who” or “that which points the way” An Index is a systematic guide (pointer) to items contained in a collection or to concepts derived from a collection. The index entries are arranged in a known or searchable order (commonly alphabetical arrangements) An Index can also be defined as a systematic guide to the location of words, concepts or other items in books, periodicals or other publications. Types of indexes are Author index, Title, subject, word index, book, periodical, citation and classified index. etc Indexing – is an operation intended to represent the results of analysis of a document by means of an indexing language.
